About Us

My Dentist, a leading dental clinic in Colombo, is a subsidiary of AME Healthcare, a well-known dental services organization based in Singapore. To ensure high-quality dental care and superior patient care, we provide exceptional support services in partnership with our clinical partners. Our network of healthcare providers in Colombo is expanding, making "Quality Smiles" available in Asia and the Middle East. Our dental care services in Colombo are backed by AME Healthcare's extensive expertise.

  • Our Mission

    At My Dentist, our mission is to provide comprehensive oral healthcare that integrates advanced technology, affordability, and unwavering integrity. We aim to lead the dental industry by setting the highest safety standards, delivering exceptional customer service, practicing ethical dentistry, and actively contributing to the well-being of our community. We are dedicated to enhancing oral health across Asia and the Middle East, one smile at a time.

  • Our Vision

    To become the most trusted dental care provider in Asia and the Middle East, recognized for our unwavering commitment to delivering high-quality and ethical healthcare.

Services

We provide comprehensive dental care to keep your smile healthy, bright, and confident..

Extraction

Safe and painless tooth removal.
Performed by experienced dentists.
Using modern dental tools.

Scaling and Polishing

Removes plaque and tartar buildup.
Polishes teeth for a smooth finish.
Promotes healthy gums and fresh breath.

Filling

A dental procedure that restores a decayed or damaged tooth by filling it with a material to prevent further decay and restore its function.

Root Canals

A dental treatment to remove infection from inside a tooth and protect it from further damage.

Dentures

emovable artificial teeth that replace missing teeth to restore your smile and improve chewing.

Braces and Aligners

Orthodontic treatments using braces or aligners to straighten teeth and improve your smile and bite.

Teeth Whitening

A cosmetic dental treatment that lightens teeth to give you a brighter, more confident smile.

Crowns and Bridges

Restorative dental treatments that cover damaged teeth (crowns) or replace missing teeth (bridges) to restore function and appearance

Dental Implants

A dental procedure that replaces missing teeth with artificial roots and crowns to restore function and appearance.

Kids Dentistry

Specialized dental care for children to ensure healthy teeth, proper oral habits, and a positive dental experience.

Periodontics

Specialized dental care focused on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of gum diseases and supporting structures of the teeth.

Minor Oral Surgeries

Dental procedures that involve minor surgeries in the mouth to treat issues such as tooth extractions, cyst removal, or soft tissue corrections.
